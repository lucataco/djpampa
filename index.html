About DJ Pampa

A professional Entertainment agency

Growing up in a family of musical entertainers, music has been my life since childhood. I started my career as a DJ in 2003 in private, corporative, social events. 2005 started spinning in clubs in the DMV area, in cities that love the taste of all various types of music. Opened concerts for Bolivian artists, experienced and collaborated in music production with rapers and singers. I also had the pleasure of being part of “Grupo Ashband” playing percussion. In 2006 I expanded my DJ career, not only was I playing at clubs but also started to host special events, weddings, engagements, birthday parties, 15 añeras, sweet 16th, etc. In 2008 I established DJ PAMPA Events, my own company providing not only DJ service but also audio and visual, which includes, professional sound system, intelligent lighting, and special effects for all occasions including corporate meetings and conventions. With over 16 years of experience in this industry I assure you that you will get world class services.
